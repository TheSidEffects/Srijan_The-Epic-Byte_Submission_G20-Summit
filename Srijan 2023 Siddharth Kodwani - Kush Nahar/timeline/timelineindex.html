TIMELINE OF EVENTS

1st December 2022

India is going to hold the G20 presidency from 1 December 2022, with its presidency's theme being Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam or "वसुधैव कुटुम्बकम्" in Sanskrit or translated as "One Earth, One Family, One Future'" in English.

4th December 2022

The 1st Sherpa Meeting of India's G20 Presidency begins today in the historic city of Udaipur, Rajasthan. Sherpa's or Presidencies get together well in advance of the summit to resolve conflicts on numerous matters.

13th January 2023

The Think20 Inception Conference was the initial gathering for India's G20 Presidency. Its purpose was to establish the objectives for Think20 India in the upcoming year. This event brought together think tanks, research institutions, and Task Force members. Over two days, there were discussions and sessions where each Task Force presented their ideas, priorities, and action plans.

16th - 17th January 2023

NEW DELHI : The first G20 Infrastructure Working Group meeting under G20 India Presidency concluded on 17 January 2023 in Pune, said the Ministry of Finance in a press release. The meeting was attended by 64 delegates from 18 member countries, 8 guest countries and 8 international organizations.

2nd - 3rd Feburary

The 1st SFWG meeting was held on February 02-03, 2023 in Guwahati, Assam. The meeting was attended in person by 95 delegates from G20 member countries, 10 invitee countries and 14 international organisations like the IMF, NGFS, World Bank, UNDP, UNEP Fi, ADB, NDB, FSB among others.

9th - 10th September

The Ministers Meeting of G20 Summit 2023 will be held in New Delhi at Bharat Mandapam International Exhibition-Convention Centre (IECC). G20 Summit 2023 New Delhi is scheduled on 9th and 10th September 2023 in which heads of 20 Countries will participate.
